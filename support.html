Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Download LeetCode Daily from the App Store or Google Play, then sign up with your Google, or Apple account.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your account and all associated data. If you have an active Pro subscription, make sure to cancel it first through your device settings.

Subscriptions & Pro

What's included in the free version?

Free users get 1 daily coding problem, progress tracking, streak counting, and access to solutions — all at no cost.

What does Pro unlock?

Pro gives you up to 3 daily problems, access to the full problem archive, all solution approaches, bookmarks, and an ad-free experience. Both monthly and yearly plans are available.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). On iOS, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. On Android, go to Google Play → Payments & Subscriptions → Subscriptions.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple or Google, depending on your device. Visit Apple's refund page or Google Play's refund page to submit a request.

Can I transfer my subscription between iOS and Android?

Subscriptions are tied to the store where they were purchased and can't be transferred directly. However, if you sign in with the same account on both platforms, your progress and data will sync.

App Features & Usage

How are daily problems chosen?

Problems are curated based on your selected skill level (Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced) and cover a wide range of algorithm and data structure topics to ensure well-rounded practice.

Which programming languages are supported?

LeetCode Daily supports Java, Python, C++, JavaScript, C#, and Go. You can change your preferred language at any time in the app settings.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Problems you've already loaded are cached locally, so you can practice without an internet connection. New daily problems require a brief connection to download.

How do streaks work?

Complete at least one daily problem to maintain your streak. Streaks reset at midnight in your local time zone. Stay consistent and watch your streak grow!

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing or not loading.

Try these steps: (1) Force close and reopen the app. (2) Check for app updates in your device's app store. (3) Restart your device. (4) Reinstall the app. If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and OS version.

I'm not receiving push notifications.

Make sure notifications are enabled for LeetCode Daily in your device settings. On iOS, go to Settings → Notifications → LeetCode Daily. On Android, go to Settings → Apps → LeetCode Daily → Notifications.

My progress isn't syncing.

Ensure you're signed in with the same account on all devices and that you have an active internet connection. Try pulling down to refresh on the home screen. If the issue continues, sign out and sign back in.
